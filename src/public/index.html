Which voice should I pick?
  • Best compatibility: Jenny and Guy (standard neural) work in every region — use these if you're unsure.
  • Best quality: the HD voices sound the most human and are ideal for long articles, but availability depends on the Azure region.
  • Multiple languages in one article: pick a Multilingual voice — it pronounces foreign names and phrases more accurately.
  • Norwegian content: use a nb-NO voice (Pernille or Finn).
