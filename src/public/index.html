Article to Speech

Paste an article URL and generate audio.

Which voice should I pick?
  • Best quality: the HD voices (e.g. en-US-Ava (HD)) sound the most human and are ideal for long articles. Availability can depend on the Azure region.
  • Multiple languages in one article: pick a Multilingual voice — it pronounces foreign names and phrases more accurately.
  • Maximum compatibility: the standard neural voices (Jenny, Guy) work in every region.
  • Norwegian content: use a nb-NO voice (Pernille or Finn).
